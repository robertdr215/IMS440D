The IMS Major with Applied Concentration is designed for students who wish to double major. 21 credit hours are required in another major. This gives students the flexibility to pursue other academic disciplines and examine how emerging technology is transforming them while still giving students a rich understanding of emerging technology.

Exposure Courses

Careers in IMS (required)
Design (all three required)
Business/Communication (three required courses plus choose one option)
  • Digital Marketing Choice (choose one)
Technology (two courses required plus choose one option)
  • Advanced Interaction Development (choose one)

Experience Courses

IMS Co-Lab (required)
Leadership and Collaboration (Internship required plus one Product Development required)
  • Product Development (choose one)
Synthesis (choose one)
